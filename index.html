Annual Report

Building leaders through discipline,unity and service.

The National Cadet Corps (NCC) at IIT Roorkee functions under the 3 UK CTR Battalion, Roorkee Group. Our unit comprises dedicated cadets committed to fostering leadership, discipline, and a spirit of national service among students.


Since its inception, NCC IIT Roorkee has played a pivotal role in shaping young minds through rigorous training, social service initiatives, and active participation in national-level camps and parades.


The unit continues to uphold the NCC motto Unity and Discipline inspiring cadets to contribute selflessly toward the nation and society.

We the cadets of the National Cadet Corps, do solemnly pledge that we shall always uphold the unity of India. We resolve to be disciplined and responsible citizens of our nation. We shall undertake positive community service in the spirit of selflessness and concern for our fellow beings.
